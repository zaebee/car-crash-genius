# Privacy Policy for CarCrashGenius

**Last Updated:** October 26, 2023

## 1. Introduction
CarCrashGenius ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website https://car.zae.life. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us for the purpose of vehicle damage analysis.

## 2. Data We Collect
*   **Uploaded Content:** Images and documents you upload for analysis.
*   **Metadata:** EXIF data (Camera model, date, location) contained within your uploaded images.
*   **AI Interactions:** Chat history and text inputs provided to the AI assistant.
*   **Wallet Address:** Your public TON wallet address if you choose to connect it.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
*   **Analysis:** Uploaded files are transmitted directly to Third-Party AI Providers (Google Cloud Vertex AI / Gemini or Mistral AI) solely for the purpose of generating the damage report.
*   **Certification:** If you opt-in, report hashes are stored on the TON Blockchain.

## 4. Data Storage
*   **Transient Processing:** We do not maintain a backend database of your uploaded photos. They are processed in memory and sent to the AI provider.
*   **Local Storage:** Your Mistral API Key (if provided) and language preferences are stored locally in your browser.

## 5. Third-Party Services
*   **Google Gemini:** Used for default image analysis. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   **Mistral AI:** Used for optional analysis. [Mistral Privacy Policy](https://mistral.ai/privacy/)
*   **TON Connect:** Used for wallet connectivity.

## 6. Contact
For questions regarding this policy, please contact us at support@zae.life.
